Q2:What is Lialina's ideal internet user? Provide some of the qualities that she outlines.

She calls the user Turing complete and says efforts are made to educate the user about herself. The ideal quality of a user is the concept of a "The General Purpose User" I think the concept of the general-purpose user is not historical thinking or temporary ideals, but the ideal user is to ultimately engage between humans and computers by the symbiosis of what we can do and what computers can do. Through true symbiosis, it is to live with computers with the mindset of a general-purpose user. It applies universal technology to users based on their needs and interests, and users have the skills to achieve their desired goals through applications or devices. In other words, it interacts with computer interface design and uses it. I think the ideal quality of a user is that a computer does not only a computer's job, but a user does not only a given job but also a universal symbiosis and uses it through interaction.
